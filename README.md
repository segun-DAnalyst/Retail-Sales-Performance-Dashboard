# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 🧠 Project Overview

RetailEdge Solutions required a scalable analytics solution to monitor sales performance across multiple cities, products and sales channels.

Sales data was fragmented across online and physical retail operations, limiting visibility into performance trends and profitability drivers.

This project delivers an **interactive Power BI dashboard** designed to centralise reporting, improve KPI visibility and support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Establish a **single source of truth** for sales analytics  
- Enable **year-over-year comparison of revenue and profit**
- Identify **top performing products, customers and cities**
- Analyse **channel contribution to overall sales**
- Improve decision-making through data-driven insights  

---

## ⚙️ Technical Approach

- Data cleaning and modelling using **Power Query**
- Creation of calculated measures using **DAX**
- Development of KPI indicators and variance tracking  
- Dashboard UX design focused on usability and interactivity  
- Implementation of dynamic filters for deeper analysis

## 📈 Key Metrics

- Total Revenue: **$155M**
- Total Profit: **$57.7M**
- Total Orders: **8K**
- Quantity Sold: **68K Units**
- Cost of Goods Sold: **$96.7M**

---

## 💡 Analytical Insights

- Christchurch generated approximately **$12M revenue**, making it the highest contributing city  
- Wholesale channel accounted for roughly **53% of total revenue (~$83M)**  
- Top revenue-generating product delivered approximately **$26M in sales**
- Top 5 customers contributed **over $18M combined revenue**, indicating revenue concentration  
- Monthly revenue remained relatively stable between **$12M–$14M**, showing consistent demand patterns  
- Weekend revenue showed noticeable fluctuation, with **Saturday experiencing lower sales (~$11M)**
